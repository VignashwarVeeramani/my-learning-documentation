# A Guide to Ethical & Cruelty-Free ETFs in Australia

For those who want to invest in line with their values, ethical Exchange Traded Funds (ETFs) offer a powerful solution. This guide lists some of the top-performing options on the Australian Securities Exchange (ASX).

Ethical investing, often called **ESG** (**E**nvironmental, **S**ocial, and **G**overnance) investing, involves selecting companies that meet certain standards of social and environmental responsibility.

For investors focused on **"ethical"** and **"cruelty-free"** principles, these funds typically screen out and exclude companies involved in:
* Fossil fuels (coal, oil, gas)
* Gambling and tobacco
* Weapons manufacturing
* Animal cruelty (e.g., live animal export, non-medical animal testing)
* Poor human rights or environmental records

## Top-Performing Ethical ETFs on the ASX

Here are some of the most popular and well-regarded ethical ETFs available to Australian investors.

### 1. **BetaShares Global Sustainability Leaders ETF (ASX: ETHI)**
ETHI is one of the largest ethical ETFs in Australia, focusing on global companies that are "Climate Leaders."
* **Investment Focus:** Global companies (excluding Australia).
* **Ethical Screening:** Excludes fossil fuels, gambling, tobacco, armaments, and has a specific screen against animal cruelty.
* **Why it's a top choice:** Offers broad international diversification with a strong ethical and performance track record.

### 2. **Vanguard Ethically Conscious Australian Shares ETF (ASX: VETH)**
VETH is a low-cost option for those wanting to invest ethically within the Australian market.
* **Investment Focus:** Diversified portfolio of Australian companies.
* **Ethical Screening:** Excludes companies involved in fossil fuels, nuclear power, alcohol, tobacco, gambling, and weapons.
* **Why it's a top choice:** A simple, low-cost "core" holding for an ethical Australian portfolio from a highly trusted manager.

### 3. **BetaShares Australian Sustainability Leaders ETF (ASX: FAIR)**
FAIR applies one of the strictest ethical screens for an Australian ETF, making it a "deep green" option.
* **Investment Focus:** Australian companies that meet very high sustainability standards.
* **Ethical Screening:** More rigorous than many others, with a strong focus on excluding fossil fuel producers and supporting companies with a positive social and environmental impact.
* **Why it's a top choice:** Ideal for investors who want to ensure their Australian investments have a significant positive impact.

### 4. **VanEck MSCI International Sustainable Equity ETF (ASX: ESGI)**
ESGI provides global exposure by investing only in companies that receive a high ESG rating.
* **Investment Focus:** International companies with leading ESG performance.
* **Ethical Screening:** Screens out controversial industries and then selects the "best in class" from the remaining companies based on their ESG score.
* **Why it's a top choice:** A great way to get diversified global exposure to companies that are actively managing their environmental and social impact.
